LexiBear – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

LexiBear is a word journal designed for parents and families to record child-invented words, meanings, audio pronunciations, and private notes.

Data Storage

All words, meanings, notes, and audio recordings are stored locally on your device.

If iCloud Sync is enabled, data is securely synchronized using Apple iCloud and CloudKit under your Apple ID account.

Data Collection

LexiBear does not collect analytics, tracking data, advertising identifiers, or behavioral information.

The developer does not have access to your dictionary content, audio recordings, or notes stored on your device or in your iCloud account.

Family Sharing & Shared Profiles

LexiBear allows you to share child profiles with invited family members through Apple’s CloudKit infrastructure.

  • Only invited participants can access shared profiles
  • The profile owner controls permissions
  • Access can be revoked at any time

Children’s Privacy

LexiBear is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. The app does not collect personal information from children.

Child profiles contain only user-created dictionary content (words, notes, and audio recordings).

Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit or delete individual words
  • Delete profiles
  • Remove audio recordings
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Delete the app to remove local data

Third-Party Services

LexiBear uses Apple system services only:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit for synchronization
  • Apple system frameworks for audio recording and playback

LexiBear does not sell, rent, or share your data.
