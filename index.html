LexiBear – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

LexiBear is a word journal designed for parents and children to record child-invented words, audio pronunciations, and related memories.

Data Collection

LexiBear does not collect, store, or process any personal data on external servers. All words, audio recordings, and related content are stored locally on the user’s device.

Third-Party Services

LexiBear offers optional AI-powered features that require the user to provide their own OpenAI API key.

When AI features are used, data is sent directly from the user’s device to OpenAI. The developer of LexiBear does not have access to, store, or process this data.

Data Sharing

LexiBear does not share user data with the developer or any third parties.

Children’s Privacy

LexiBear is designed to be used by parents or guardians. The app does not collect personal information from children.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LexiBear collect my data?

No. All data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to the developer.

How do AI features work?

AI features are optional and require your own OpenAI API key. Requests are sent directly from your device to OpenAI.

Does LexiBear support Apple Family Sharing?

Yes. The subscription supports Apple Family Sharing. Availability depends on your App Store subscription settings.
