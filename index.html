LexiBear – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

LexiBear is a word journal designed for parents to record child-invented words, meanings, audio pronunciations, and notes.

Data Storage

All data (words, meanings, notes, and audio recordings) is stored locally on your device.

LexiBear does not require account creation and does not transmit personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

LexiBear does not collect analytics data, tracking information, advertising identifiers, or behavioral data.

The developer does not have access to your content stored on your device.

Children’s Privacy

LexiBear is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. The app does not collect personal information from children.

Child profiles contain only user-created content (words, notes, and audio recordings).

Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit or delete words
  • Delete profiles
  • Remove recordings
  • Delete the app to remove all local data

Third-Party Services

LexiBear uses only Apple system frameworks for core functionality such as audio recording and playback.

LexiBear does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
